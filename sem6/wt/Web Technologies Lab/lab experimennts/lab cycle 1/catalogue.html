Description of Departments

    1. CSE
    2. IT
    3. ECE
    4. MECH
  • CSE

    The department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 1986. The department is committed to imparting quality education and producing highly skilled graduates who are in demand in the industry. Currently, the department offers B. Tech. (Computer Science & Engineering), M. Tech. (Computer Science & Technology) and Ph.D. Program along with Certificate Programme on Programming in Python.

  • ECE

    The department started in the year 1981, became autonomous in 2003 and evolved as a constituent department in GITAM Institute of Technology, GITAM from 2007. The department is NBA accredited twice in the years 2002 and 2006. Students passout from this department with good placements and progression statistics. The department has highly qualified faculty with high retention and varied specializations. It is well equipped with State-of-Art Laboratories.

  • IT

    Established in the year 2000 , the department of Information Technology offers B.Tech (IT), M.Tech (IT) and Doctoral programme. All the programs of the department are accredited with ‘A’ grade by NAAC. The curriculum is designed to cater the needs and demands of IT industry. The department has 1200 undergraduate students , 60 Postgraduate students and 43 research scholars working under department faculty. The department has state–of-art labs and infrastructure to facilitate students in acquiring hands on experience in addition to classroom teaching.

  • MECH

    Mechanical Engineering Department is one of the oldest departments in GITAM, Established in the year 1980. Mechanical Engineering is one of the major activities in the engineering profession and its principles are involved in the design, study, development and construction of nearly all of the physical devices and systems. Continued research and development have led to better machines and processes helping the mankind.
